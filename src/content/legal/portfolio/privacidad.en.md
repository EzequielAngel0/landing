This Privacy Policy describes the data practices of the portfolio website of Angel Ezequiel Barbosa Lomeli, available at `portfolio.angelezequiel.dev` (the "Site").

**Summary:** the Site is static, has no accounts or forms, uses no cookies and collects no personal data. Analytics are aggregate and cookie-free.

## 1. Data controller

The Site is owned by Angel Ezequiel Barbosa Lomeli (Jalisco, Mexico). Contact: barbosalomeliangelezequiel@gmail.com.

## 2. Data collected

The Site requires no registration, includes no forms that send data to a server, and creates no user profiles. No name, email or contact details are collected through the Site.

## 3. Cookies

The Site uses **no cookies** or equivalent technologies for tracking or advertising.

## 4. Cookie-free analytics

To measure visits in aggregate, the Site uses **Cloudflare Web Analytics**, a tool that **uses no cookies**, stores no identifiers in your browser, and does not track people across sites. Metrics are aggregate and cannot identify you.

## 5. Hosting and delivery network

The Site is served through GitHub Pages and Cloudflare (delivery network and DNS). Like any web service, their servers may log connection data (for example, IP addresses) temporarily for security and operational purposes. That processing is governed by those providers' own policies.

## 6. Third-party links

The Site links to external services (for example, GitHub, LinkedIn, X or WhatsApp). When you follow those links you leave the Site, and each service's own privacy policy applies.

## 7. Your rights

Since no identifiable personal data is collected through the Site, there is no processing over which to exercise access or deletion rights against the owner. For any question you can use the contact in section 9.

## 8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated. The "Updated on" date indicates the latest revision.

## 9. Contact

For questions about this policy, write to barbosalomeliangelezequiel@gmail.com.
