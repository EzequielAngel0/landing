This Privacy Policy describes the data practices of the `angelezequiel.dev` website (the "Site"), the personal hub of Angel Ezequiel Barbosa Lomeli that links to his portfolio and social profiles and hosts the legal pages of his applications.

**Summary:** the Site is static, has no accounts or forms, uses no cookies and collects no personal data.

## 1. Data controller

The Site is owned by Angel Ezequiel Barbosa Lomeli (Jalisco, Mexico). Contact: barbosalomeliangelezequiel@gmail.com.

## 2. Data collected

The Site requires no registration, includes no forms that send data to a server, and creates no user profiles. No name, email or contact details are collected through the Site.

## 3. Cookies and local storage

The Site uses **no cookies** or equivalent technologies for tracking or advertising. The only information it keeps in your browser is your theme preference (light or dark), stored locally (localStorage) for a purely functional purpose: it never leaves your device and is not used to identify or track you.

## 4. Analytics

The Site does not use cookie-based analytics. The only measurement, if active, is **Cloudflare Web Analytics**, a tool that uses no cookies, stores no identifiers in your browser, and does not track people across sites; its metrics are aggregate and cannot identify you.

## 5. Hosting and delivery network

The Site is served through GitHub Pages and Cloudflare (delivery network and DNS). Like any web service, their servers may log connection data (for example, IP addresses) temporarily for security and operational purposes. That processing is governed by those providers' own policies.

## 6. Third-party links

The Site links to external services (portfolio, GitHub, LinkedIn, X, WhatsApp and email). When you follow those links you leave the Site, and each service's own privacy policy applies.

## 7. Legal documents of the applications

The Site hosts the privacy policies and terms of Angel's applications (for example, SoloKey). Each of those documents governs the data practices of its own application; this policy governs only the `angelezequiel.dev` website.

## 8. Your rights

Since no identifiable personal data is collected through the Site, there is no processing over which to exercise access or deletion rights against the owner. For any question you can use the contact in section 10.

## 9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated. The "Updated on" date indicates the latest revision.
