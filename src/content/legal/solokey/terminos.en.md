## 1. Description of the service

SoloKey is a **local-first** password manager for Android and for the Windows desktop companion. It lets you create and organize credentials, passwords, secure notes, API keys, TOTP codes, SSH keys and secure files, encrypted and stored on your own devices. Device-to-device sync is optional and works peer-to-peer over your local network.
